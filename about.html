⚡ Established 29th November 2007

Nearly two decades of powering homes

Shree Mahalaxmi Electricals was incorporated on 29th November 2007, and for close to two decades since, we've been proud to serve the people of Kalaburagi with genuine, ISI-marked electrical products — from geysers and wires to switches, fans, MCBs, and lighting.

What hasn't changed in all these years is why we started: a belief that every customer, whether a homeowner rewiring a single room or an electrician stocking up for ten sites, deserves the same honesty, the same quality, and the same care.

Every product on our shelves is chosen the way we'd choose it for our own home — because in a business built on wires and current, trust is the one thing that can never be cut short.
